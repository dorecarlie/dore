Doré is a fashion app dedicated to make casting models for certain shoots easier.
The purpose of this app is to keep things organized and helps bring spotlight to upcoming models to get
exposed into the fashion world as well. With this app, users can sign up either as a model, or a casting
director. As a model, they are able to upload posts to their online portfolio such as headshots for example. They are
also able to have a small bio, as well as their location, contact information and measurements. This is put
